Introduction

In 2014, United Nation High Commisioner for Refugees (UNHCR) Jordan and PeaceGeeks began partnership to snable refugees and services providers to quickly access up to date information on all services available to refugees. A key challenge facing all stakeholders was accessing up to date information on available services at any given moment in time. Until recently, there has been no single place for considering this information. Services information changes constantly because of changing pririties.

Service Advisor

Service Advisor is a web app that connects refugees to essential information on all humanitarian services. It helps refugees to essential information on all humanitarian services. It helps refugees, service providers and coordinating bodies, which provides up-to-date information about local availability of humanitarian services. The app allows users to view available services on a map and in a service directory, where they can filter by category/sector, region, service provider and referral requirements.
